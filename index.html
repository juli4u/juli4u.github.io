REPLICATOR

Michah Dowbak (aka Mad Dog Jones) is a multidisciplinary artist from Thunder Bay, Ontario. He created the this art piece. Titled REPLICATOR, the NFT artwork is a 50-second looped video that tells the story of a machine through time. The artwork depicts the accelerating wave of development through a digital image of a photocopier in a Los Angeles office space and demonstrates how fast recent technologies are quickly going into obsolescence.

EVERYDAYS: THE FIRST 5000 DAYS

Everydays: the First 5000 Days is a digital work of art created by Mike Winkelmann, known professionally as Beeple. Digital NFT Everydays: the First 5000 Days is a mosaic of his first 5,000 vector drawings and animations. Winkelmann was inspired by British artist Tom Judd and began the daily project on 1 May 2007. Some of the images involve figures from pop culture, including Jeff Bezos and Donald Trump, and are arranged chronologically. Some of the earlier images are hand-drawn and not computer-produced.

REPLICATOR

Michah Dowbak (aka Mad Dog Jones) is a multidisciplinary artist from Thunder Bay, Ontario. He created the this art piece. Titled REPLICATOR, the NFT artwork is a 50-second looped video that tells the story of a machine through time. The artwork depicts the accelerating wave of development through a digital image of a photocopier in a Los Angeles office space and demonstrates how fast recent technologies are quickly going into obsolescence.

EVERYDAYS: THE FIRST 5000 DAYS

Everydays: the First 5000 Days is a digital work of art created by Mike Winkelmann, known professionally as Beeple. Digital NFT Everydays: the First 5000 Days is a mosaic of his first 5,000 vector drawings and animations. Winkelmann was inspired by British artist Tom Judd and began the daily project on 1 May 2007. Some of the images involve figures from pop culture, including Jeff Bezos and Donald Trump, and are arranged chronologically. Some of the earlier images are hand-drawn and not computer-produced.

STAY FREE

Stay Free (Edward Snowden) is an NFT artwork that shows how NFTs could be put to multiple uses. The NFT depicts images of National Security Agency whistleblower Edward Snowden and pages of a court of appeal decision that ruled the mass surveillance program Snowden exposed had violated U.S laws.

OCEAN FRONT

Beeple, a graphic designer from Charleston, SC, whose real name is Mike Winkelmann created this Nft art piece. The piece depicts the state of climate change and the precarious state of the world by showing minivans and trailers balanced on scaffolding, and a tree rests at the very top. It also makes up part of the record-breaking Everyday collection.

CROSSROADS

Beeple, a graphic designer from Charleston, SC, whose real name is Mike Winkelmann created this Nft art piece. The NFT, titled “Crossroad,” depicts former President Trump lying in a heap after losing the 2020 presidential election. The work represents a dynamic art piece in the sense that it can change form and shape and is one of the earliest examples of dynamic art pieces.

FREEROSS: PERSPECTIVE

Created by the incarcerated Ross Ulbricht, founder of the notorious Silk Road marketplace, the "Perspective" NFT and its surrounding collection sparked both fascination and debate. For some, "Perspective" symbolizes Ulbricht's own experience, trapped within prison walls yet striving for hope and freedom. The upward gaze echoes a yearning for a brighter future, perhaps mirroring Ulbricht's ongoing legal appeals and fight for clemency.

RINGER #879 (THE GOOSE)

Ringers #879 (The Goose), part of the Ringers Art Blocks collection created by Dmitri Cherniak. The meaning of "The Goose" specifically is open to interpretation, as with most abstract art. Some viewers might find the concentric circles mesmerizing or meditative, while others might see them as symbolic of growth, expansion, or infinity. The title "The Goose" could add another layer of meaning, potentially evoking associations with flight, freedom, or even silliness. Ultimately, the meaning of the artwork is up to the individual viewer to decide

CLOCK

Clock is an NFT art by anonymous digital artist Pak. The NFT, like its name, is a clock which counts the days WikiLeak’s founder Julian Assange has been locked up in London’s Belmarsh Prison.

CRYPTOPUNK #8857

CryptoPunk #8857, one of the 10,000 unique pixel art characters created by Larva Labs in 2017. It depicts a male punk with wild, spiky hair and 3D glasses, making it one of the 88 zombie punks in the collection. The pixelated style and unconventional appearance of the punk could be seen as a rejection of mainstream ideals and a celebration of nonconformity. The 3D glasses, a hallmark of CryptoPunks, could be interpreted as a nod to the increasing role of technology in our lives and the blurring lines between the physical and digital worlds.

THE EVERLASTING BEAUTIFUL

FEWOCiOUS, a semi-anonymous 18-year-old artist from Las Vegas named Victor made this NFT art piece. The meaning of "The Everlasting Beautiful" remains a treasure chest hidden within the beholder's heart. Its interpretation dances to the rhythm of individual experiences and cherished beliefs. The artist's vibrant palette and whimsical style, born from childhood innocence and imagination, offer a glimpse into a world where even in the shadows of a global pandemic, hope and beauty persist, like embers refusing to be extinguished.

FOREVER ROSE

Forever Rose is created by Kevin Abosch. The singular rose in this piece pulsates with symbolic meaning. Its crimson hue, a timeless emblem of love and passion, stands in stark contrast to the void-like black, amplifying the intensity and permanence of affection's grip. Abosch invites viewers to contemplate the nature of love, prompting questions of whether its essence, like the artwork itself, can truly ever fade.
